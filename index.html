Problem

  • Annually 8.8 lakh children die from starvation with over 20 crore Indians sleeping hungry daily resulting in 7,000 daily hunger-related deaths, totaling 25 lakh yearly.

    •  

  • Globally 25,000 including 10,000 children die daily from hunger; yearly toll 20 million, 3.1 million children.

    •  

  • In India, 30-40% of food (70M tonnes/year) is wasted, while globally 1/3 of total production (2.5B tonnes/year) is wasted.

    • Solution

  • To bridge the gap between surplus food sources and individuals or families facing food insecurity.

    •  

  • We're dedicated to rescuing surplus food from various outlets and redistributing it to those in need.

    •  

  • Through our efforts, we aim to alleviate hunger, minimize food waste, and empower communities to thrive.

    • Impact

  • Thousands of pounds of surplus food rescued annually

    •  

  • Millions of nutritious meals provided to individuals and families facing food insecurity

    •  

  • Reduction of environmental impact through decreased food waste

    •  

  • Empowerment of communities through shared resources and support

  • How we are working :-

    • Our platform allows businesses, restaurants, event organizers, and households to easily report surplus food by specifying type, quantity, and

    preferred pickup time. Our dedicated team swiftly collects the food, ensuring safety, and distributes it to those in need through partnerships with

    local food banks, shelters, and community centers. Together, we turn excess food into a powerful solution for hunger and food waste.
